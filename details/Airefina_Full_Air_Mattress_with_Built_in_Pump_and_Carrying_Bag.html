Airefina Full Size Air Mattress

Looking for a versatile and comfortable sleeping solution? The Airefina Full Size Air Mattress is designed to deliver a bed-like experience in a compact and easy-to-use format. With dimensions of 75x54x18 inches, it offers ample space for a restful night's sleep, whether you're staying overnight at a friend's house or preparing for a camping adventure.

This inflatable mattress features a built-in pump for a quick inflate/deflate time, making it simple to set up or store. Its waterproof material ensures easy cleaning, and the durable construction keeps it lasting through frequent use. The non-slip bottom provides stability, making it safe for both adults and children to use.

For added comfort and support, the Airefina includes 28 internal air coils that help maintain proper spinal alignment. Pair it with the included carrying bag and puncture repair patch for hassle-free travel and long-term reliability. Whether you need a quick sleep solution at home or a reliable option for outdoor adventures, this mattress is the perfect choice.
