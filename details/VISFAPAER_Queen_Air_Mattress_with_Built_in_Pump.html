VISFAPAER Queen Air Mattress - Your Ultimate Comfort for Any Adventure

The 18-inch height of this mattress provides a balanced sleeping posture, helping to reduce pressure on your knees and back. Its raised structure offers a floating bed sensation, making it feel like a luxurious, real mattress with every night.
