Castle Squire Medium Firm Memory Foam Lumbar Pillow

Designed to provide exceptional back support for your home, office, or travel needs, this lumbar pillow is your ultimate companion for better posture and comfort. With its high-density 70D memory foam, it adapts perfectly to your lower back, helping to maintain proper alignment and reduce strain during long hours of sitting or lying down.

Featuring an orthopedic wave shape, the pillow targets the natural curves of your spine, offering a balanced blend of support and relaxation. The 100% vegan polyester cover is not only soft against your skin but also machine washable and easy to maintain, making it a practical choice for everyday use. A convenient zipper allows for quick removal and storage, so you can keep your pillow clean and ready whenever you need it.

Whether you're working at your desk, driving to your destination, or relaxing on the couch, the Castle Squire Lumbar Pillow delivers consistent support in every setting. The adjustable strap ensures a secure fit, adapting to different seat widths and keeping the pillow in place without any hassle. Its 3D mesh construction enhances breathability, keeping you cool and comfortable throughout the day.

Perfect for individuals who suffer from back pain or simply want to improve their posture, this pillow combines functionality with sleek design to elevate your overall comfort experience.
